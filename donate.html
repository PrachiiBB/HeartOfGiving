Sponsor a ​Pink Room

Pink Room in schools will provide special care, comfort, and relief to women and girls by encouraging them to follow good hygiene practices especially their menstrual hygiene. Contribute towards building such Pink Rooms in schools so that young girls do not drop from school due to lack of proper sanitation facilities.

There are many bright children who do not have access to quality education due to their underprivileged status. Sponsor a bright underprivileged child and help them achieve overall academic excellence.
