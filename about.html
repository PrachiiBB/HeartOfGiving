About Heart of Giving

Heart of Giving is a nationally recognized Non-profit that was established in the year 2016 and inspires, empowers, and aims to uplift underprivileged children by introducing a culture of giving and bringing about a positive change in the lives of these children and the society at large. The organization is the brainchild of Antony Sajeeth, a mentor, sponsor, counselor and coach, who has dedicated his life towards uplifting needy and underprivileged children by helping them excel in studies and sports to build a better future for themselves and contribute towards the society.

Our initiatives are centered around both academics and sports and the results have been very encouraging. While Sajeeth is the face of Heart of Giving, the various initiatives are not possible without the support of our generous donors and volunteers who contribute from time-to-time in our projects.

Story behind Heart of Giving

Antony Sajeeth, the force behind establishing Heart of Giving, has had 25 years of experience in the field of IT services and gaming industries and donned many hats as an administration and facilities professional. Sajeeth was always inspired by his childhood hero, Dr. HT Sangliana Retd. ADGP and Former Commissioner of Police, Bangalore City, to walk the extra mile to serve the society.

Since his college days, Sajeeth was always a part of numerous social causes and initiatives. Having worked closely with various NGOs in the past, he has realized that a targeted approach is needed to be impactful and drive the change you want to see in the society.

This is how Heart of Giving came into existence. The organization was established in the year 2016 and is now a registered Non-Profit under Section 8 of Companies Act.

Sajeeth’s ideology to use empowering aspects of sports to facilitate social change is inspired from home from his daughter who is an athlete herself! He believes sports does a great job of inculcating values and life lessons that go a long way in moulding children’s lives and hence he chose to work with children. Our focus has been in uplifting children in Govt. Schools in rural areas.

Objective

  • Provide access to good quality digital education
  • Promote sports to train talented students to be a part of sports competitions
  • Encourage overall academic performance by providing scholarships to bright students
  • Provide basic infrastructural facilities at Govt. schools
  • Promote good hygiene practices amongst girl students

Our Approach

Children have great potential to direct and dictate social change in their lives as well as inspire the next generations. We work directly with children, supporting them to overcome their economic and social limitations, giving them a chance at a brighter life.

Heart of Giving follows this 3-point approach in every project it begins:

Tangible Contributions:

We accept tangible contributions like cash rewards, scholarships, sports equipment, computer lab & table-chair setup for equipping underprivileged children with quality education and sports facilities. All our contributions are visible, accounted for and much needed.

Measurable Impact

Securing top positions in sports competitions, reaching higher targets in academics or balancing both, we know what motivates children to turn into consistent performers and the results speak for themselves.

Follow Ideas to the End

From promptly delivering on promises of cash rewards and scholarships to computer training sessions, regular follow-ups on the progress and assisting each project till the very end is our key motive.
