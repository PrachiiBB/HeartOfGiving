Our Projects

Pink Room

The Pink Room is an essential sanitation infrastructure that has been set up for women and young girls who come to school. The sanitary room provides special care, comfort, and relief to these girls and encourages them to follow good hygiene practices and improve their sanitation habits.

Read More

Sponser A Child

This program focuses on improving the well-being of children in need, especially the most vulnerable. The program gives them a chance at a brighter life by encouraging them to be a part of national level competitions, promoting higher education, and focusing at the overall academic performance.

Read More

Facilitate Sports Infrastructure

Sports is a great way to build your strength and mental development. It does a great job of inculcating values and life lessons that go a long way in helping you become an all-rounder. The focus is to achieve this by facilitating quality sports infrastructure in Govt. schools in rural areas.

Read More

Scolarship For Toppers

Education cannot take a back-seat. And this becomes the reason behind Heart of Giving’s two-pronged approach. Our initiatives are centered around both academics and sports and the results have been very encouraging.

Read More

Computer Lab Setup

In today’s digital world, technology-driven education is extremely important. Despite this, most rural schools lack access to a computer and internet facilities. To bridge this gap we are initiating e-learning by setting up computer labs in government schools.

Donation Campaigns

Sponser a Pink Room

Pink Room in schools will provide special care, comfort, and relief to women and girls by encouraging them to follow good hygiene practices especially their menstrual hygiene. Contribute towards building such Pink Rooms in schools so that young girls do not drop from school due to lack of proper sanitation facilities.

Sponser a Bright Underpriviledged Kid

There are many bright children who do not have access to quality education due to their underprivileged status. Such children if given a chance to study at proper schools will go a long way in fulfilling their dreams and achieving success. Sponsor a bright underprivileged child and help them achieve overall academic excellence.

Infrastucture of Govt Schools

Most of the Govt. schools, especially in rural areas lack basic school infrastructure. This forces children to drop out from schools at a young age. Donate towards developing remarkable infrastructure at Govt. schools including quality sports infrastructure.
